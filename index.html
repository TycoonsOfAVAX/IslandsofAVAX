About the Bridges of AVAX

Ready to be collected and traded

The Bridges of AVAX is a collection of 901 NFT bridges on the Avalanche (AVAX) network. Bridges of AVAX varies with different tiers, traits and comes with a uniqute quote or interesting facts about them. Bridge levels are determined by economic capacity, popularity and how widely they're used. (Will be useful at the next phase.)

Bridgeonomics

Limited Supply

Only 901 bridges will ever be minted. Bridges are minted in multiple phases and distributed to the AVAX community. The first batch is known as GENESIS bridges.

Different Levels of Rarity

Not all bridges are the same, there are 5 levels of rarity of bridges: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Ultra Rare and Legendary. Each level with an exponentially decreasing supply count.

Free Launch

Unlike other NFT projects that charge an arm and a leg to mint. The team behind Bridges of AVAX decided to airdrop most of the GENESIS bridges to lucky NFT collectors.

Future Functions

All NFT bridges have traits which affects their tolling power and economic activity, a figure that will be used extensively in the next phase of development (blockchain game).
