About the Islands of AVAX

Ready to be collected and traded

The Islands of AVAX is an archipelago of islands rich full of untapped resources and developmental opportunites. Island cities are eager for food and resources and there are research rich islands waiting to be collected. Will you preserve their natural beauty or will you build industries to meet the growing needs of the island cities?

Islandnomics

Unique Islands

Each island is unique and has its own island number, resource type, resource output and location.

Rarity

Certain islands are more rare due to the resources, size, characteristics which may yield different levels of economic potential in future stages of the game.

Develop and power up

While islands initially are undeveloped, players may opt to erect resource extraction facilities on their island based on the island type. (eg. Forest island = Lumber Mill, Mountain island = Mine)

Project DAO (Proposed)

A portion of the sale proceeds will go towards a perpetual fund. Monthly revenue from the perpetual will go towards the simluation of the economic activity (Eg. Token Airdrops & other NFT airdrops)

Roadmap

Prelaunch

Islands of AVAX will be launching with a number of prize giveaways on social media. Be sure to follow us on Twitter and Telegram to not miss out.

Launch

We will be launching on NFTrade.com. Islands will be minted and released in batches, priced from 1 AVAX each and are claimed on a first come first serve basis.

Economic Simulator (Proposed)

A gaming fund made of a portion of the presale revenue will be put into an interest yielding platform (eg. AAVE/CRV etc...). Revenue from the DAO gaming fund will be distributed to island holders to simulate economic activity (specifics to be announced).

District and Federal Regional Governance

All islands are classified to specific district and federal regions, players may opt to be distict or regional governors and receive a % of ecomoic activity of all players within their region. They also can set policies from taxes to developmental incentives as well as construct district infrastructures to improve overall district economic output.
