Welcome, Freelancer Captain.

AVAX-pelago has fallen to the FIATland foreign occupiers, the grand AVAX Navy has been destroyed in the final battle and the regional government has capitulated. AVAXpelago enters into a new chapter as the populance decides on whether they submit to the FIATland way of life or resist and work towards liberation. As a freelance captain, this is a prime time for you to make a fortune.

Battles are fought with $SHIP tokens which are generated every month based on the # of island and shipyards you have. Win missions to earn $RP, Resource Points, to power up your fleet, the resistance movement and make major breakthroughs against the FIATland occupation.

Resistance Naval Yard

Things have changed a lot since then. The FIATland armada has moved on to the Fantom region and left behind a modest garrison to bring about stability in the region. We no longer build ships from a centralized location but instead individual islands are building ships in a decentralized manner at their local shipyards. Battles/mission will be determined by $SHIP tokens with rewards coming in the form of $RP (resource Points).

Collect Your Ships

Every month, shipyards and islands create vessels ($SHIPs) for the resistance cause. Each player's claimable $SHIP allotment are calculated at the beginning of each month and players can claim up to their alloted $SHIP count. Unclaimed ships will expire by the posting of the next calculation. $Ship calculations for each turn (month) are as follows (subject to change):

  • Each shipyard/Island will generate a base of 0.1 $SHIP
  • Having a pair of shipard + Island will give you a synergy base bonus of 1 $SHIP for each pair you hold
  • Governor Islands will grant governors 10 $ships
  • Resistance Powerups when built will give an exclusive boost to owner of the powerup NFT and a general boost to all players
  • Clan/Company powerups will give an exclusive boost to owner of the powerup NFT and all faction members
Players can do as they like with their $SHIPs from committing them to the mission of the month or hoarding them up for a future mission or selling them on the secondary market (If players establish a secondary market on their own).
