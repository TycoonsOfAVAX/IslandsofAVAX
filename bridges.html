Bridgeonomics

Free Launch

Unlike other NFT projects that charge an arm and a leg to mint. The team behind Bridges of AVAX decided to airdrop most of the GENESIS bridges to lucky qualifying NFT collectors. Some were dropped to qualifying GB HODLers during prelaunch while others are set apart for the general launch drop and social media giveaways.

Limited Supply

Only 901 bridges will ever be minted. Bridges are minted in multiple phases and distributed to the AVAX community. The first batch is known as GENESIS bridges which are dropped to qualifying GB holders and members of the community. The price of the bridges will be determined by the community.

Different Levels of Rarity

Not all bridges are the same, there are 5 levels of rarity of bridges: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Ultra Rare and Legendary. Each increasing level will have an exponentially decreasing supply count. Bridges will eventually be allowed to be traded in for an upgrade on a 5 to 1 ratio.

Future Functions

Bridges of AVAX started off with the love for bridges from which a community was established. As you may be aware, the NFT bridges are minted with traits which affect their tolling power and economic activity (A potential feature that may be implemented in the future.).
